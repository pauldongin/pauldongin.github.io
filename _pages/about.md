I am a 3rd-year PhD student in the [Department of Kinesiology](https://www.lsu.edu/chse/kinesiology/) at [Louisiana State University](https://www.lsu.edu/), advised by [Dr. Senlin Chen](https://www.lsu.edu/chse/kinesiology/faculty_staff/chen.php). My research focuses on understanding and promoting healthy behaviors in children and adolescents, with a particular emphasis on:

- **Physical activity** promotion and measurement
- **Sedentary behavior** and **screen time** reduction
- **Childhood obesity** prevention and intervention

I am currently working on a summer camp intervention study aimed at promoting physical activity and reducing sedentary behavior among school-aged children.

## Publications

I have published work in journals including *PLOS One* and *Frontiers in Sports and Active Living*. See my full list of publications [here](/publications/).
